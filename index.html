Rusty Football

This was the final project for my Engineering & Economic Financial Management class. The goal was to build a profitable collection of bets on NFL games. This project consisted of two parts:

  1. A quadrinomial tree implemented in Rust with each step representing each drive in an NFL game. This structure was inspired by the binomial tree method of pricing options.
  2. A forecasting model to predict the performance of an NFL team based on their performance in the previous year.
